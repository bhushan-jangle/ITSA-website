Question no:-1

Lady Han, ’Court Lady of the Royal Kitchen’ knows the number from 1 to 20 . But she says that she knows the number from 1 to 150. She remembers the number, greater than 20 in such a way, that she writes a number in a four digit number combination. From where when she divide the main number by 1st three numbers, she could get 4th number as common remainder. for ex. 33 as (5, 10, 15, 3) Once she went to the market and she had Rs. (3, 5, 7, 2) in her pocket. One of her friend offered Rs.(5, 10, 15, 3) She bought fruits of Rs. (10,12,15,6) and vegetables of Rs. (9, 12, 18, 6) find how much money she left.
(C, C++, java )
( no need to take any extra input through keyboard )

Question no:-2

A guy wants to celebrate birthday of his girlfriend. But he don’t know actual date of her birthday and he can’t ask her. So help him to find out her birth-date, which is on first Monday of **/****(Month/Year). Also find her age in ****(Year) and on which day her birthday will fall in same year.
( C, C++, java)
( Use of any inbuilt function is strictly restricted, so use calculative method only.)
a) Input : Month and Year(through keyboard),Year should be less than 2017.
b) Assume and declare any variable if required.
c) **** Input Year from user.

Question no:-3

An army troupe get stuck somewhere.They want to contact with ‘Army Head Control Office ‘, and make them aware about it. They are using a system which is based on the amplitude modulation technique. They send their location where they stuck in the format of 32,37,47,_,_,_ but due to noise, few signals get lost during transmission. Control center get the information in the form of 1’s and 0’s (Bit stream) after adding carrier frequencies of 52 kHz, 32 kHz, 33 kHz, 22 kHz, 14 kHz and 03 kHz respectively to the original signal.
Finally Head Office receives their location. Show all the signals and conversions and find out that location.
( C, C++, java )
( no need to take any extra input through keyboard )

Brainiac of The Year - 2016

The event was organised on 13 September 2016. Respected Principal Sir Dr. S. B. Somani Sir, Former HOD of our department Dr. S. S. Prabhune Sir, faculty members Prof. M. D. Kalbhor, Prof.F. I. Khandwani, Prof. A. G. Sharma were the chief guest of our event. Total 144 students participated in the event. Our Vice Chairperson Miss. Nikita Mankar welcomed all the participants. 4 phases were held in the event. Every phase was conducted by our ITSA members. 1st phase included questioning round from which 40 students were selected for 2nd round. 2nd phase included balloons and questioning round from which 8 students were selected for 3rd round. 2 students were selected from Wild Card entry. 3rd phase was based on some creative ideas from which 3 students were selected for final round. Final phase was based on some clues and a story from which winner was selected. Mr Shantanu Gawande was the winner of our event who was awarded a cash prize of Rs. 1000/- by our Principal Sir.
